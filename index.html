About

Have you ever wondered what your life would look like if you quit your job and launched yourself onto a new path? Do you feel that you’re not being true to your core values? We all have ambitions, desires, and dreams. How many of us act on them? The list of things stopping you from doing so can be long: family, responsibility, fear of failure. I know the list well because I let each of these things stand in my way.

Then, after more than 20 years in the corporate world, I made the break. And I’ve never looked back. I’ve always known that I’m a nurturing person. The path I chose is one of helping others to achieve their dreams.

If you’re interested in making a change, contact me. I’ll work with you in a safe, trusting environment, at a pace you set.

Services

Individual Coaching Sessions

Individual coaching provides a new way forward, with an unbiased brainstorming partner. It’s a space where you can think differently in an exploratory and engaging way.

Duration: Individual sessions of 30, 60 or 90 minutes

Professional Coaching Packages

Packages are intended for areas where more time and focus are required, than what a few individual sessions might cover. The current coaching packages on offer are based on the most frequently sought out types of coaching from my clients. However, we are all unique with individual needs and the focus will depend on you. If you have something else in mind, let’s talk!

Duration: 12 or 24 sessions
