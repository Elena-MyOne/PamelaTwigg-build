Why Coaching

Professional coaching involves a uniquely collaborative relationship between a coach and an individual or group that aims to enhance participants’ personal and professional growth, improve their performance, and achieve their goals. Where therapy may delve deeply into the past, coaching is focused on the present and future, and involves helping individuals clarify their vision, identify their strengths and weaknesses, set specific and measurable goals, and develop action plans to achieve those goals.

Professional coaching can benefit individuals at all levels of an organization, from executives to managers to employees, and for individuals in transition who are looking to make a personal or professional change. In working with clients, I employ a variety of tools and techniques to unlock potential, develop self-awareness, gain clarity and focus, and increase confidence.

Services

Individual Coaching

Individual coaching provides an opportunity to brainstorm about a challenge that has recently emerged, where a new way forward is desired. It’s a space where you can think differently in an exploratory and engaging way and often is looking for a new insight to an existing challenge. These work well with clients where a rapport has already been established through longer-term engagements as individual sessions tend to be short notice and ad-hoc in nature.

Startup and Small Business Coaching

Are you considering starting your own business? Do you feel like your entrepreneurial spirit is calling but you don’t know how to get started? Coaching can help you explore what your startup will look like and find the practical steps to make it a reality.

Maybe you’re already a small business owner and you’re at a crossroads. Often, people who run their own company had initial success by letting things develop organically but reach a point where they no longer feel in control.

Accountability Coaching / Time Management

Does it feel like you have several plates spinning and at any moment one of them can come crashing down? That’s natural, but it doesn’t have to continue. Accountability coaching clarifies your vision, helps you set priorities and goals, and provides the direction to achieve those goals.

Self Confidence / Imposter Syndrome

Almost everyone will experience anxiety and a lack of self-confidence at some point in their lives regardless of how outgoing and self-assured you might have been in the past. When it occurs, it often can be unsettling and has us question all that we know to be true about ourselves. Learning to manage this and channel it in other ways to keep it from managing us is something we can work on together.

Leading and Managing Change

Are you leading or managing change through inspiration, creating trust, and generating enthusiasm? Or do you find yourself leading change through email directives to avoid dealing with the difficult questions? How you show up with your team and how you lead transition can either be empowering or debilitating for those around you. Learning how and what you say and how you lead transition is something we can work on together.

Public Speaking and Anxiety

If you are experiencing anxiety associated with public speaking or feel that it is keeping you from reaching your full potential, you are not alone. Public speaking anxiety is a common fear that many people experience, including CEOs of global companies, performers, and well-seasoned speakers. Learning to manage this through techniques and practice is something we can work on together.

Empowering Others / Delegating Tasks

Are you finding it hard to let go of tasks that you have quickly and easily done in the past? Does delegating feel like you are giving up control? Empowering others and delegating tasks are essential components of effective leadership and team management. By learning how to delegate more, you can increase productivity, develop skills, foster teamwork, reduce stress, and encourage creativity within your team. This is something we can work on together.

Difficult and Challenging Conversations

Do you avoid difficult and challenging conversations with your team, colleagues, peers, family, or friends? Are you concerned that what you have to say will create unwanted emotional responses or perhaps be interpreted wrong? Do you feel that there is a cost to you personally for not being able to have those challenging conversations? Learning how to confidently address difficult and challenging conversations through techniques and practice is something that we can work together.

Effective (Virtual) Communication

How you communicate is as important as what you communicate. People pay attention to verbal and nonverbal cues. Do you feel that you struggle to know what to say when? Effective communication is essential for success in both personal and professional life. It helps individuals and organizations achieve their goals, build strong relationships, and work collaboratively to solve problems and create new ideas. Together we can work on improving your communication skills through techniques and practice.

Empowering Others / Delegating Tasks

This cutting-edge methodology is based on 10 Archetypes, which are underlying patterns of human behavior. As such they are – often invisibly – “running the show” as we go about our lives. These Archetypes are embedded in our motivations and values. The better we understand these patterns and the more consciously we use them, the more effective we will be. Archetypes at Work™ offers an exciting framework to understand how people act and “what is really going on” – in our life, in our work, and in our deeper self.

I am a certified debriefer and Archetypes at WorkTM Guild Member and highly recommend this as a way for clients to assess how they are showing up today vs. how they might need to show up to be more in alignment with their purpose at work and life. It is a positive approach to personal development. More information is available on Archetypes at Work - Evolving Your Story, One Character at a Time.

Common Approaches for individual Services

Each client is unique and has an idea of what they would like to focus on during the sessions. This is discussed and agreed on during the first session. Depending on the desired outcome, we can apply tools that will:

  • Help you to rediscover and affirm your core values.
  • Reveal your strengths and identify areas for growth.
  • Help you cope with unexpected roadblocks.
  • Build confidence.

Here are some of the common approaches that might be useful for clients. Please note that these are merely suggestions and are not meant to limit the client. If there is a topic that you would like to explore and don’t see it in the list, please contact me and we can see what’s possible to accommodate your needs.

1 x 90-minute discovery session

10 x weekly or bi-weekly 30–60-minute sessions

1 x 60-minute wrap up and feedback session

24-sessions program:

1 x 60-minute wrap up and feedback session

Benefits of professional coaching

Some of the many tangible and proven benefits of professional coaching are:
  • Increased self-awareness and confidence
  • Greater wellbeing
  • Increased productivity
  • Accelerated learning
  • Positivity
  • Return on Investment
  • Improved relationships

I will have access to various tools and methods, including self-assessment tools, reflection techniques, listening, and “powerful questioning,” to name a few. These tools are available for the individuals to use to move themselves forward and achieve their goals.
