Services

Individual Coaching Sessions

Individual coaching provides a new way forward, with an unbiased brainstorming partner. It’s a space where you can think differently in an exploratory and engaging way.

Duration: Individual sessions of 30, 60 or 90 minutes

Professional Coaching Packages

Packages are intended for areas where more time and focus are required, than what a few individual sessions might cover. The current coaching packages on offer are based on the most frequently sought out types of coaching from my clients. However, we are all unique with individual needs and the focus will depend on you. If you have something else in mind, let’s talk!

Duration: 12 or 24 sessions

Professional Coaching Packages

Accountability Coaching

Does it feel like you have several plates spinning and at any moment one of them can come crashing down? Are you pulled in many directions because you feel that you can’t say no? Accountability coaching clarifies your vision, helps you set goals, and provides the direction to achieve your goals.

As your accountability coach, my main job is to keep you in check and provide the required motivation so you can achieve your goals within specified deadlines.

Duration: 12 or 24 sessions

Startup and Small Business Coaching

Are you considering starting your own business? Do you feel like your entrepreneurial spirit is calling but you don’t know how to get started? Coaching can help you explore what your startup will look like and find the practical steps to make it a reality.

Maybe you’re already a small business owner and you’re at a crossroads. Often, people who run their own company had initial success by letting things develop organically but reach a point where they no longer feel in control.

Duration: 12 or 24 sessions

12-sessions program:

1 x 90-minute discovery session

10 x weekly or bi-weekly 30–60-minute sessions

1 x 60-minute wrap up and feedback session

24-sessions program:

1 x 90-minute discovery session

22 x weekly or bi-weekly 30–60-minute sessions

1 x 60-minute wrap up and feedback session

Benefits of professional coaching

Some of the many tangible and proven benefits of professional coaching are:
  • Increased self-awareness and confidence
  • Greater wellbeing
  • Increased productivity
  • Accelerated learning
  • Positivity
  • Return on Investment
  • Improved relationships

I will have access to various tools and methods, including self-assessment tools, reflection techniques, listening, and “powerful questioning,” to name a few. These tools are available for the individuals to use to move themselves forward and achieve their goals.

I adhere to the ICF Code of Ethics
