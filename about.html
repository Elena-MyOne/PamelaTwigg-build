About Me

Pamela is a Professional Executive and Personal Coach based on the East Coast of the United States. Her generative wholeness approach to coaching brings clarity, direction, and positive support for her clients. By identifying where growth is desired, setting goals and objectives, and encouraging accountability, she helps clients to become empowered to learn how to obtain their goals and live balanced lives. Her own personal and professional experiences — of living on two continents and the challenges she faced in building and rebuilding a new life — are woven into the coaching she provides. Pamela coaches remotely and in person, depending on clients’ preferences and locations.

Pamela has more than 20 years of experience in international business, mainly in the ICT sector, working in the United States and in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. Most recently, she has listened to what her inner voice, gut and heart have been saying and quit her corporate job to focus full-time on her coaching business. Since then, she hasn’t looked back.

Pamela holds a bachelor’s degree in Modern Languages from Clarion University and a master’s degree in American Studies from the University of Amsterdam. She is an accredited Associate Certified Coach with the International Coaching Federation, a certified generative coach through inviteChange and a certified Archetypes at Work TM guild member. And speaking of Archetypes, her leading Actors are Explorer, Lover and Sovereign.

Pamela lives in beautiful Western Maryland with her husband, 2 of the 6 children in her blended family, as well as their ever so energetic dog, Friso. She is fluent in English and Dutch and moderately comfortable in French. She is currently training for her second half marathon, which she plans to run in the fall with her son. Wish her luck!
